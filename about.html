About Us

Lubombo Thinkpulse Technologies is a South African-registered technology research and engineering company founded in April 2026. Established with a vision to transform and improve the places we live, the company is dedicated to developing innovative technologies that solve real-world challenges through science, engineering, and innovation.

Our Vision

To transform and improve the places we live by solving everyday challenges through technology, science, and innovation, creating a smarter, more sustainable future for Africa and the world.

Our Mission

To identify real-world challenges and develop innovative, practical, and sustainable technologies that improve lives, empower industries, and contribute to the advancement of society through continuous research, engineering excellence, and strategic collaboration.

Our Values

Our culture and innovation are guided by these principles.

Innovation Integrity Excellence Sustainability Reliability Collaboration African Innovation
